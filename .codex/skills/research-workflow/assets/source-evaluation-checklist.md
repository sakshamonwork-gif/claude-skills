## Credibility Indicators
- [ ] Author has verifiable domain expertise
- [ ] Published in a reputable outlet (peer-reviewed journal, major newspaper, official org)
- [ ] Date is recent enough for the topic's pace of change
- [ ] Cites primary data, studies, or other authoritative sources
- [ ] Funded / written independently (no clear commercial bias)

## Quality Signals
- [ ] Claims are evidence-based (not purely opinion)
- [ ] Multiple perspectives are considered
- [ ] Methodology or data sources are clearly explained
- [ ] Conclusions are proportionate to the evidence presented
- [ ] Has been cited by or is consistent with other credible sources

---

## Overall Rating
- 🟢 **High credibility** — passes 8+ checks above
- 🟡 **Medium credibility** — passes 5–7 checks
- 🔴 **Low credibility** — passes fewer than 5 checks (use with caution; verify elsewhere)
